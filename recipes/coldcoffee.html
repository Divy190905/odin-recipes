Cold Coffee's Recipe

"Ingredients"

  • 1 cup chilled milk
  • 1 tablespoon instant coffee powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 4-5 ice cubes
  • Optional: 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
  • Optional: Whipped cream for topping

"Instruction"

  1. In a blender, add the chilled milk, instant coffee powder, sugar, and ice cubes.
  2. Blend until the mixture is smooth and frothy.
  3. If using vanilla ice cream, add it to the blender and blend again until smooth.
  4. Pour the cold coffee into a tall glass.
  5. Top with whipped cream if desired.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
