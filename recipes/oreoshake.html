Oreo Shake's Recipe

Ingredients

  • 6 Oreo cookies
  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream (optional, for garnish)
  • Additional Oreo cookies (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Place the Oreo cookies in a blender and pulse until they are finely crushed.
  2. Add the vanilla ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract to the blender.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour the shake into glasses.
  5. Top with whipped cream and additional crushed Oreo cookies, if desired.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
