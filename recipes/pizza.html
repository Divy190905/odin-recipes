Pizza's Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110째F/45째C)
  • 3 1/2 to 4 cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, olive oil, salt, and sugar. Add the yeast mixture and mix until a dough forms.
  3. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for about 8-10 minutes, until the dough is smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 to 1 1/2 hours, or until it has doubled in size.
  5. Preheat your oven to 475째F (245째C).
  6. Punch down the dough and divide it into two equal pieces. Roll out each piece into a 12-inch circle on a floured surface.
  7. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over each pizza base.
  8. Sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese and add your desired toppings.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  10. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly before slicing and serving.
